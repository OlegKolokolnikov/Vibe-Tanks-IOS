# Privacy Policy for Vibe Tanks

**Last Updated:** December 18, 2024

## Overview

Vibe Tanks ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Oleg Kolokolnikov. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in relation to the App.

## Information Collection

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App:
- Does not require user registration or login
- Does not collect personal data (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not make network requests

## Data Storage

All game data (such as high scores and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any servers and is not accessible to us.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The game is suitable for players of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
